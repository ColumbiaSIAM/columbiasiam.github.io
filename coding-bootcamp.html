Coding Bootcamp

What

The first annual Coding Bootcamp, held on the campus of Columbia University, will be teaching Python to high school students (9th-12th grades). The program will prepare participants for the Moody's Mega Math Challenge (https://m3challenge.siam.org/). Participation in the M3 Challenge is recommended but not required.

The program is entirely FREE! Food is provided. Transportation is NOT provided.


When

Six sessions, 3 in the fall and 3 in the spring.

Fall Dates: Oct 23, Nov 13, Dec 4

Spring Dates: TBD


Where
Mudd Building 12th Floor Botwinick Lab
