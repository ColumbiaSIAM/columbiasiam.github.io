Coding Bootcamp

What

The 3rd annual Coding Bootcamp, held on the campus of Columbia University, will be teaching Python (and some Matlab) to high school students (9th-12th grades). This NRHH-recognized program will prepare up to 50 participants for the MathWorks Math Modeling Challenge ( https://m3challenge.siam.org/) organized by SIAM. Participation in the M3 Challenge is recommended but not required.

The program is entirely FREE! Transportation is NOT provided. You don't have to bring anything other than yourself to the sessions. We especially encourage students who have made the most (not necessarily technically) out of the opportunities they have been given, students who identify themselves as part of an underrepresented group, and students from different schools to apply.


When

Six sessions, 3 in the fall and 3 in the spring.

2018/19 Dates: 11/11, 11/18, 12/2

Day/Time: Sunday, 12:00 pm to 4:30 pm


Where
