Coding Bootcamp

What

The 2nd annual Coding Bootcamp, held on the campus of Columbia University, will be teaching Python to high school students (9th-12th grades). The program will prepare participants for the Moody's Mega Math Challenge (https://m3challenge.siam.org/). Participation in the M3 Challenge is recommended but not required.

The program is entirely FREE! Transportation is NOT provided. You don't have to bring anything other than yourself to the sessions. Experience is NOT required.


When

Six sessions, 3 in the fall and 3 in the spring.

Fall Dates: Oct 22, Nov 12, Dec 3

Spring Dates: Feb 4, Feb 25, Mar 25

Time: 12:00 pm to 4:30 pm


Where
Mudd Building 12th Floor Botwinick Lab
