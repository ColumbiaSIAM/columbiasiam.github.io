About Us

  • Workshops

    Teach and learn at Latex, Matlab, and Python workshops!

  • Networking

    We hold networking events to connect people! There's a First-generation Low Income Students in STEM Networking Event, an Underrepresented in STEM Networking Event, and a Women in STEM Networking Event.

  • Tutoring

    We organize Python Coding Bootcamp for local high school students, with funding from Google.

    https://columbiasiam.github.io/coding-bootcamp.html

  • Join Us

    Join us and help us organize our workshops, networking events, and tutoring efforts. Also enjoy our weekly newsletter, research symposiums, speakers, and panels, help rooms, and field trips.

    https://columbiasiam.github.io/joinus.html
